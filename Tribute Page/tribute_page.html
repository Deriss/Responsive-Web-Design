Dr. René Favaloro

René Gerónimo Favaloro (1923-2000) was a worldwide known Argentinian cardiosurgeon. His main medical contribution was the development of the coronary bypass procedure for the cirurgical treatment of arteriosclerosis. He was also one of the founders of the "Fundación Favaloro" in 1975, a clinical center for cardiopaties' treatment that combines pacient's treatment, medical reseach and education.

René Favaloro suicided on the 29th of July, 2000, by shoting himself in the heart due to economic problems of the Fundación and a discontent with the corruption of the health system in his country.
